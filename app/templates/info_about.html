About
Nexus Health

We’re building the cleanest, fastest way for patients and caregivers to get the durable medical equipment they need with AI automation, trusted suppliers, and a patient-first ethos.

Restore your lifestyle

We exist with one mission; we will do any and everything we can to work for you. Our mission is to do our best to restore your lifestyle.

Since 1962, we have been restoring our patients’ lifestyles for 60 years. We believe the process of getting Durable Medical Equipment and Medical supplies should be a safe, comfortable, and, in some cases, a fun experience.

We are a family-owned business, and we believe families must be there for each other. You deserve to be treated as our family.

We did not survive on our own for 60 years; we believe that our survival is because of the love and support from the community.
